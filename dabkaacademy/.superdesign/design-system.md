# DABKA ACADEMY - Brand & Design System

> Palestinian Dabka Dance Education Platform - Streetwear meets Cultural Heritage

---

## The Story
Dabka Academy teaches the iconic Palestinian traditional folk dance through an online video course and nationwide pop-up classes. We're preserving Palestinian cultural heritage while making it accessible to the diaspora and anyone who wants to learn. Every step carries the rhythm of our ancestors, every movement tells our story. This isn't just dance—it's resistance, celebration, and identity in motion.

## Color Palette (Palestinian Flag Inspired)

| Color | Hex | Usage |
|-------|-----|-------|
| **Palestinian Red** | `#CE1126` | Primary accent, CTAs, energy, passion |
| **Palestinian Green** | `#007A3D` | Secondary accent, success states, hope |
| **Deep Black** | `#1a1a1a` | Headlines on light, footer backgrounds |
| **Clean White** | `#fafafa` | Primary backgrounds, breathing room |
| **Soft Gray** | `#6b7280` | Body text, muted elements |
| **Border Gray** | `#e5e7eb` | Subtle borders, dividers |

### Color Psychology
The Palestinian flag colors create instant cultural recognition. Red for passion and resistance. Green for hope and growth. Black for the land. White for peace and purity. These aren't just colors—they're a statement.

### Color Rules
- Primary backgrounds are ALWAYS white `#fafafa` (light theme)
- Headlines use black `#1a1a1a` for maximum impact
- Red `#CE1126` for all primary CTAs and accent elements
- Green `#007A3D` for secondary accents, success states, badges
- NEVER use blue, purple, or cold colors
- High contrast is key—this is streetwear, not minimalist tech

## Typography

### Primary: Inter/System Sans (Bold weights)
- Headlines: Font weight 900 (Black), uppercase, tight tracking
- Body: Font weight 400-500, normal tracking
- Clean, modern, works at all sizes

### Hierarchy
| Role | Weight | Size Range | Style |
|------|--------|------------|-------|
| **Hero Headlines** | 900 | 48-96px mobile, 80-150px desktop | UPPERCASE, tight tracking (-0.02em) |
| **Section Headlines** | 800 | 36-48px mobile, 48-72px desktop | UPPERCASE, tight tracking |
| **Subheadlines** | 600 | 18-24px | Normal case or uppercase |
| **Body Text** | 400 | 16-18px | Normal, relaxed line-height |
| **CTAs** | 700 | 14-18px | UPPERCASE, wide tracking |
| **Labels/Badges** | 600-700 | 12-14px | UPPERCASE, extra wide tracking |

### Typography Rules
- Headlines are ALWAYS bold/black weight
- UPPERCASE for impact on hero sections
- Tight tracking on big headlines (-0.02em to -0.03em)
- Wide tracking on small text labels (0.1em+)
- Line heights: Headlines 0.9-1.0, Body 1.5-1.7

## Visual Style

### The Vibe
Supreme x Palestine. Brutalist typography meets cultural pride. Bold, unapologetic, modern.

### Key Elements
1. **Massive typography** - Headlines that dominate the viewport
2. **High contrast** - Black on white, red accents that pop
3. **Geometric shapes** - Clean lines, sharp edges, no rounded corners on main elements
4. **Tatreez patterns** - Subtle Palestinian embroidery motifs as decorative accents
5. **Palestinian flag stripe** - As borders, dividers, or accent lines
6. **Negative space** - Let the typography breathe

### Photography Style (when used)
- High contrast, desaturated with selective color pops
- Documentary/authentic feel, not polished stock photos
- Movement and energy captured
- Cultural authenticity—real dabka, real people

### What We DO
- Bold, massive headlines that command attention
- Sharp geometric layouts
- Palestinian flag colors as primary palette
- Cultural patterns (tatreez) as subtle textures
- High contrast, clean compositions
- Streetwear-inspired graphic design

### What We DON'T Do
- Soft, feminine aesthetics
- Pastel colors or gradients
- Rounded, bubbly design elements
- Decorative script fonts
- Generic stock photography
- Corporate/enterprise look

## UI Components

### Buttons
- Primary: Red `#CE1126` bg, white text, no border-radius (sharp corners)
- Secondary: Green `#007A3D` bg OR transparent with border
- Hover: Slight lift (translateY -2px) + shadow
- Always uppercase text, bold weight, wide tracking

### Cards
- White background, subtle border `#e5e7eb`
- Hover: Border changes to red `#CE1126`
- Sharp corners (no border-radius or minimal)
- Generous padding

### Navigation
- Clean, minimal, fixed top
- Logo left, links center or right
- Mobile: Hamburger with full-screen takeover menu
- Palestinian flag stripe accent possible at top

### Accent Elements
- Red accent bars (small rectangles below headlines)
- Palestinian flag stripe dividers (black/white/green/red)
- Tatreez-inspired geometric patterns as backgrounds

## Page Templates

### Hero Section
- Full viewport height
- Massive stacked headline (2-3 lines)
- Eyebrow text in green or muted color
- Primary CTA in red, secondary as outline
- Subtle background texture or pattern

### Feature Grid
- 2-3 column on desktop, single on mobile
- Icon + bold title + description
- Consistent padding and spacing
- Hover states with red accents

### Pricing/CTA Section
- Centered layout
- Price prominent in large text
- Feature checklist with checkmarks
- Single primary CTA
- Trust badges below

### Footer
- Dark background (black `#1a1a1a`)
- White/gray text
- Palestinian flag stripe at top
- Social icons
- Newsletter signup

## Animation & Interaction

- Subtle hover lifts on interactive elements
- Smooth transitions (200-300ms)
- No excessive animation—confident, not playful
- Scroll-triggered fade-ins are OK but minimal
- Mobile: Ensure all touch targets are 44px+

## Brand Assets

- "DABKA ACADEMY" text logo
- "DABKA" in black, "ACADEMY" in red (or vice versa)
- Clean sans-serif, ultra-bold
- Can be stacked or inline

## Anti-Patterns (Never Do This)

- Corporate blue color schemes
- Rounded, soft, "friendly" aesthetic
- Stock photo people in generic poses
- Gradients (solid colors only)
- Script or decorative fonts
- Cluttered layouts
- Generic "online course" template look
- Anything that could be mistaken for a yoga studio
